Bio

Hi! I am a Research Scientist at Snap Research in Palo Alto, where I work on generative models. My team and I are looking for interns. Feel free to reach out to me directly as well!

Before joining Snap, I was a postdoc at Stanford CS and the Wu Tsai Human Performance Alliance where I worked closely with professors Serena Yeung, C. Karen Liu, and Scott Delp. I did my Ph.D. at the University of Toronto advised by professor Rich Zemel.
