\section{Research Interests}
My current research focuses on \textbf{personalized generative AI}.  My goal is to \textit{(1)} build tools that allow users to tell their personal stories, and \textit{(2) }find scientific insights about identity of real people.

% My research revolves around the integration of generative capabilities into perception systems.  
% Probabilistic generative models are well-suited for solving real-world problems where the model is required to \textit{complete partial input} data (e.g., forecasting trajectory or 2D-to-3D lifting for 3D reconstruction). They also allow us to build more \textit{robust and reliable} methods by better estimating the distribution of the input or the distribution of model parameters.  
% My recent research contributions include \textbf{3D computer vision} and \textbf{open-world learning}.
